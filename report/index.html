To restore your password and Master key:

1. Go to the forgotten data recovery page https://payeer.com/auth/?forgot_password=yes

2. Enter your account number, email or phone number you used to create your account with.

3. The new password is not sent to your email or phone, so please save it after creating. You will receive the master key only (if you set it in the account settings).
For @live.com, @hotmail.com and other mail from Microsoft please add support@payeer.com in this list https://col128.mail.live.com/mail/options.aspx?subsection=6 (Options > Safe and blocked senders > Safe senders) and try again.

For @tiscali.it please contact to tiscali.it support with this error: delivery temporarily suspended: host etb-2.mail.tiscali.it[213.205.33.63] refused to talk to me: 421 cmgw-4.mail.tiscali.it ESMTP server temporarly not available

Most likely your computer is affected by Trojan. The stolen funds are withdrawn from the System by the hackers immediately as general, thus it is usually impossible to return them.

Please do not use the same password for your Payeer account and other sites.
1. Change password to your account https://payeer.com/ru/account/settings/?tab=updatepass

2. Set the authorization confirmation with telegram (free) or sms (0.05 USD per sms) and turn on the master key that was sent to your email when creating the account https://payeer.com/account/settings/?tab=security

3. Change your email password and set two-phase authorization:
  • @gmail.com http://www.google.com/landing/2step/
  • @mail.ru https://e.mail.ru/settings/security#TwoStepAuth
  • @yandex.ru https://yandex.ru/promo/2fa

4. Check your computer for sofrware viruses http://www.kaspersky.ru/antivirus-removal-tool

5. Turn off all the extantions (including flash and java plugins) for the browser you use for Payeer.

Please note that as long as you use Windows OS or login with Android your account can be hacked at any time, regardless whether you have antivirus software or not. For the safe work with your electronic wallets and banks we strongly recommend you to purchase another PC and set the Ubuntu or Mac OS. 99% of viruses and Trojans can affect Windows OS only. This is the most trustworthy and very effective way to avoide hacking.
